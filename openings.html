Research student position

We are always looking for highly motivated students with a passion for solving challenging problems in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence to join our research team. If you are eager to contribute to cutting-edge research, please submit your application through the Doctoral College at the University of Plymouth. To express your interest in working with me, please also send an email to me with your CV and a brief outline of your research interests.

Funding Opportunities

If you think you have a great idea, we would be happy to support your research proposal under the a range of fellowship opportunities. Some of the available options are listed below:

  • Marie Curie Individual Fellowships
  • Leverhulme Trust Early Career Fellowships
  • Newton International Fellowships
  • Commonwealth Scholarships and Fellowships
  • Croucher Scholarships for Doctoral Study from Hong Kong
  • Inlaks Shivdasani Scholarships from India
  • Scholarships and Funding options at University of Plymouth

To maximize your chances of securing the fellowship, please contact me well in advance of the application deadlines. We can discuss potential research projects within our team and provide guidance on your application. When contacting, please provide a detailed overview of your research interests along with a copy of your CV highlighting your expertise.
